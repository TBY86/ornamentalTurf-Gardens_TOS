1. Information We Collect

Ornamental Turf & Gardens ("we," "us," or "our") collects information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Request a quote or consultation for landscaping services
  • Schedule a turf installation or garden maintenance appointment
  • Sign up for our seasonal lawn care newsletter
  • Opt-in to receive SMS updates about your project
  • Contact our customer service team

This information may include: your name, address, email address, phone number, property details, service preferences, and any other details you choose to share about your landscaping needs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our landscaping services:

  • Provide turf installation estimates and garden maintenance services
  • Communicate project updates, appointment confirmations, and service reminders
  • Improve our customer experience and service quality
  • Send promotional offers or seasonal maintenance tips (only with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations including A2P 10DLC SMS requirements

3. SMS Messaging & A2P 10DLC Compliance

When you provide your phone number and check the SMS consent box on our forms, you explicitly agree to receive text messages from Ornamental Turf & Gardens. These messages may include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Project status updates for your landscaping work
  • Seasonal lawn and garden care tips
  • Limited promotional offers (maximum 4 messages per month)

Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP to any message to opt out of SMS communications. For help, reply HELP.

We do not share or sell your phone number to third parties for marketing purposes. Your consent to receive SMS is not a condition of purchasing any services.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With vendors who help operate our business (payment processors, scheduling software) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • SMS Delivery Platform (GoHighLevel): Your mobile phone number and SMS consent records are shared exclusively with our SMS messaging platform (GoHighLevel) solely for the purpose of delivering the text messages you have explicitly consented to receive. This sharing is limited to message delivery and opt-out management. Under no circumstances is your mobile information shared for any marketing or promotional purposes by this platform.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition, with notice to you, provided the acquiring entity agrees to honor these same privacy restrictions regarding mobile information.

Important A2P 10DLC Compliance Notice: Your mobile opt-in data and consent records are never shared with any third party for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information is used exclusively to facilitate the specific SMS communications you have authorized.

Text messaing originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except for aggregators and providers of the Text Messaging services.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Opt out of SMS marketing by replying STOP to any message
  • Unsubscribe from emails by clicking the link in our communications
  • Request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are directed to adults (property owners, business owners). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
